About the Event

The Suncoast Developers Conference is a free community event put together to highlight Tampa Bay's developers and share knowledge with each other.

About US

Suncoast Developers Guild is a collective of software engineers, programmers, and designers in Tampa Bay. With thousands of members across dozens of local groups, our members touch all aspects of technology development in Tampa Bay. The Academy at SDG is a code school that serves people, not profit. We are changing lives and teaching people to be the best software developers they can be.

Our mission is to provide those seeking an education in software development with the technical and soft skills they need to pursue rewarding careers as programmers. We also seek to promote a sense of community among local technology organizations, supporting members in a way that strengthens our community and promotes the common good.
